Frequently Asked Questions

What criteria do you use to select startups?
We look for innovative startups with a strong team, scalable business model, and potential for market impact. While we consider early-stage startups, we prefer those with at least a prototype or MVP.
How long does the application process take?
The initial application review takes about 2-3 weeks. If selected for the next round, you'll be invited for interviews and pitches, which may take an additional 2-3 weeks.
Is there any cost to join the program?
There's no cost to apply or participate in our startup program. We invest in promising startups in exchange for equity, typically between 3-7% depending on the stage and amount of support required.
What kind of mentorship can I expect?
You'll be matched with mentors based on your industry and specific needs. Our mentors include successful entrepreneurs, industry experts, investors, and executives from leading tech companies.
Do you provide funding?
Yes, we provide seed funding to selected startups. Additionally, we help connect you with our network of investors for subsequent funding rounds.
